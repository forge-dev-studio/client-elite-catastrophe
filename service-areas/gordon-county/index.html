Tree Service Across Gordon County

Gordon County sits along the I-75 corridor north of Rome, built around Calhoun and the smaller river towns like Resaca. The Oostanaula and its feeder creeks run wooded bottoms through the county, and those damp, low-lying stands are where clay stays soft the longest. On the higher ground, established neighborhoods hold mature water oaks and red oaks with limbs reaching over rooftops and drives, while the fast-growing corridor near the interstate keeps leaving tall loblolly pines exposed after the graders move on. Both patterns fail in the ways we see everywhere in Northwest Georgia: hardwoods hollowing out unseen, pines uprooting in saturated ground, sweetgums dropping wood in a stiff wind. Elite Catastrophe covers Gordon County on a regular schedule from Rome, bringing the same crew and equipment we use at home for removals, pruning, hazard checks, and stump grinding. When severe weather crosses the county, our storm crews respond 24/7.

We also cover the smaller communities of Gordon County, including Fairmount, Plainville, Ranger. If you are anywhere in the county, call and tell us where you are.
