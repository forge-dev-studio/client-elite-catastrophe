Storm Damage & Emergency Tree Service in Woodstock, GA

Woodstock grew from a small railroad town into one of the largest suburbs in Cherokee County, and the older sections kept their trees the whole way. Heavy hardwood canopy shades the established neighborhoods, while the fast-growing subdivisions around them hold the tall pines left behind when construction cleared the trees that once sheltered them. That is a lot of wood over a lot of rooftops, and North Georgia storms know how to find it. When straight-line winds and hail move through, the failures come in a hurry: a loblolly uprooted out of saturated clay and laid across a driveway, an oak limb punched through a garage, a whole tree leaned onto the power line. That is when Elite Catastrophe gets the call. We are a family-owned company based in Rome, serving Woodstock and the surrounding Cherokee County. Storm response leads what we do here, handled structures first: the trees that have hit homes and vehicles or blocked roads come first, in the order that gets people safe and moving again. Those emergency calls are answered around the clock and year round, through the night and through the weekend, because that is when the wind does its damage. If a falling tree opens the roof, we tarp it so the next band of rain stays outside. Once the emergency passes, the same crew handles the everyday list any time you need it: tree removal, tree trimming, stump grinding, and hauling off what the storm cracked or killed. The storm is what most people call about in Woodstock, but a questionable tree over your roof on a calm week is reason enough too.

What we bring to Woodstock

  • 24/7 emergency tree service, when a storm drops a tree on your home
  • Storm damage restoration, from the tree to the roof it landed on
  • Emergency roof tarping, to stop the water until repairs are made
  • Tree removal, storm-driven or scheduled, any time of year
  • Tree trimming and pruning, to keep the canopy off your roof
  • Stump grinding and removal, to finish the job clean

Everyday tree work in Woodstock is available year round, not only after a storm. When severe weather does hit, we are the crew that answers fast and works structures first.
