Storm Damage & Emergency Tree Service in Waleska, GA

Waleska is a small, rural town in Cherokee County, best known as the home of Reinhardt University and wrapped in the kind of wooded, open acreage that gives big trees plenty of room to grow. That same room means a lot of tall timber standing close to homes, barns, and drives, and when a storm hits Waleska, all that wood becomes the problem. The failures follow the regional script. Loblolly pines, shallow-rooted in red clay that softens after days of rain, snap off or heave out of the ground when straight-line winds hit. Old oaks and other hardwoods shed heavy limbs or come apart at the trunk. On rural lots the fallen tree often takes a fence, a shed, or a stretch of driveway with it, and sometimes the power line goes down under the weight. Elite Catastrophe is a family-owned company based in Rome, serving Waleska and the rest of Cherokee County. Storm response leads the way, and structures first is how we work: trees off houses, vehicles, and roads before anything else, with emergency calls answered around the clock and year round because storms here do not keep office hours. When a tree tears open the roof, we tarp it to keep the inside dry until repairs can be made. After the urgent work, the everyday services stay available on your schedule: tree removal, tree trimming, stump grinding, and storm damage cleanup so the property is usable again. Waleska's headline is storm response, but the same crew is glad to look at a tree on an ordinary afternoon, long before the radar lights up.

What we bring to Waleska

  • 24/7 emergency tree service, when a storm drops a tree on your home
  • Storm damage restoration, from the tree to the roof it landed on
  • Emergency roof tarping, to stop the water until repairs are made
  • Tree removal, storm-driven or scheduled, any time of year
  • Tree trimming and pruning, to keep the canopy off your roof
  • Stump grinding and removal, to finish the job clean

Everyday tree work in Waleska is available year round, not only after a storm. When severe weather does hit, we are the crew that answers fast and works structures first.
