Storm Damage & Emergency Tree Service in Nelson, GA

Nelson sits right on the Cherokee and Pickens county line, a small town in old marble country where the surroundings are rural and heavily wooded. It is a quiet place most of the year. Then North Georgia's storm season arrives, and the quiet gets interrupted by the sound of trees coming down. The setup around Nelson is all tall timber and open ground. Loblolly pines stand in red clay that turns soft after a long soaking rain, and when straight-line winds and hail push through, those pines snap or lift out at the roots. Mature hardwoods fail too, dropping big limbs or splitting where the trunk forks. On these rural properties a falling tree tends to find something: a roofline, a vehicle, a driveway, or the power line running out to the house. When that happens, Elite Catastrophe answers. We are a family-owned company based in Rome, serving Nelson and the north end of Cherokee County. Storm response is our lead work, run structures first: the trees down on homes, cars, and roads come off ahead of anything else, and emergency calls are answered around the clock and year round. If the roof is punctured, we tarp it to hold back the next round of weather. Once the immediate danger is cleared, the same crew takes care of the rest whenever it suits you: tree removal, tree trimming, stump grinding, and cleanup of the wood a storm leaves behind. In a rural, wooded town like Nelson the storm is the reason most people call, but a declining tree near the house is worth handling on a calm day, well before it picks its own moment.

What we bring to Nelson

  • 24/7 emergency tree service, when a storm drops a tree on your home
  • Storm damage restoration, from the tree to the roof it landed on
  • Emergency roof tarping, to stop the water until repairs are made
  • Tree removal, storm-driven or scheduled, any time of year
  • Tree trimming and pruning, to keep the canopy off your roof
  • Stump grinding and removal, to finish the job clean

Everyday tree work in Nelson is available year round, not only after a storm. When severe weather does hit, we are the crew that answers fast and works structures first.
