Storm Damage & Emergency Tree Service in Kennesaw, GA

Kennesaw grew up in the shadow of its mountain, and the wooded subdivisions filling in around it are heavy with trees. That is beautiful most of the year and a genuine problem for about twenty minutes at a time, when a North Georgia storm line comes through and the wind funnels across the ridges. Elite Catastrophe is a Rome-based, family-owned crew serving Kennesaw, and when a storm hits this part of Cobb County we answer the phone at two in the morning and get on the road. The tree profile in Kennesaw's newer neighborhoods is the classic setup for wind damage. Tall pines that grew up sheltered inside a stand, then were left standing alone after construction cleared the trees around them, now rise exposed in compacted red clay. Add a saturated spring and a hard gust, and those are the pines most likely to end up horizontal, sometimes through a bedroom. Mixed in are maturing hardwoods over the older parts of town that shed heavy limbs under the same conditions. When we roll into Kennesaw after a storm, structures come first: trees on houses, trees on cars, trees blocking a road or a driveway, with roof tarping on hand when a limb has punched through. That storm response is the headline, but it is not the whole service. Removals, tree trimming, storm damage cleanup, and stump grinding are available around the clock and year round, storm or calm, for any homeowner who wants a hazard handled before the weather decides it. The wind-prone trees below Kennesaw Mountain do not wait for a convenient day, and neither do we.

What we bring to Kennesaw

  • 24/7 emergency tree service, when a storm drops a tree on your home
  • Storm damage restoration, from the tree to the roof it landed on
  • Emergency roof tarping, to stop the water until repairs are made
  • Tree removal, storm-driven or scheduled, any time of year
  • Tree trimming and pruning, to keep the canopy off your roof
  • Stump grinding and removal, to finish the job clean

Everyday tree work in Kennesaw is available year round, not only after a storm. When severe weather does hit, we are the crew that answers fast and works structures first.
