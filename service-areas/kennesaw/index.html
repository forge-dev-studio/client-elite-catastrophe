Storm Damage & Emergency Tree Service in Kennesaw, GA

Kennesaw grew up in the shadow of its mountain, and the wooded subdivisions that fill in around it are heavy with trees. That is beautiful most of the year and a real problem for about twenty minutes at a time, when a North Georgia storm line comes through and the wind funnels across the ridges. Elite Catastrophe is a Rome-based, family-owned storm-response crew, and Kennesaw is one of the Cobb County towns we deploy to when severe weather leaves trees where they do not belong. We are honest about the arrangement. We do not work these streets every day. What we do is answer the phone at two in the morning when a loblolly pine has snapped off at forty feet and come down across a bedroom, and then get on the road. The tree profile in Kennesaw's newer neighborhoods is the classic setup for wind damage. Tall pines that grew up sheltered inside a stand, then were left standing alone after construction took away the trees around them, now stand exposed in compacted red clay. Add a saturated spring and a hard gust, and those are the trees most likely to end up horizontal. Mixed in are maturing hardwoods over the older parts of town that drop heavy limbs under the same conditions. When we deploy to Kennesaw after a storm, structures come first: trees on houses, trees on cars, trees blocking a road or a driveway. From there we handle removals of what cannot be saved, cut back the broken and hanging limbs, and grind the stumps once the yard is safe to work in. The storm is the reason to call. Everything else follows it.

What we bring to Kennesaw

  • 24/7 emergency tree service, when a storm drops a tree on your home
  • Storm damage restoration, from the tree to the roof it landed on
  • Emergency roof tarping, to stop the water until repairs are made
  • Tree removal, for the hazards a storm leaves standing
