Storm Damage & Emergency Tree Service in Smyrna, GA

Smyrna packs a lot of house and a lot of tree into a small footprint. The Jonquil City is one of the denser inner suburbs of Cobb County, with mature street trees lined up close over sidewalks, driveways, and rooftops that sit only a few feet apart. That closeness is exactly what makes storm damage here so unforgiving: when a tree comes down in Smyrna, there is rarely open ground for it to land on, so it lands on something built. Elite Catastrophe serves Smyrna from our Rome base, and we make the drive because a family in a tight neighborhood with a tree through the roof needs a crew that does this work day in and day out. The mature street trees that give Smyrna its character are also the ones that fail into houses. Older water oaks and red oaks carry heavy limbs over rooflines, and when hail and straight-line winds work them over, or the red clay softens under a week of rain, big wood comes down in the worst possible place. Taking trees off structures in a dense neighborhood is precision work: limbs come down in pieces, in the right order, with no room for error between two close houses. When a storm hits Smyrna we handle the emergency first, tarp any opened roof to keep the rain out, then clear the storm damage. And the calmer work is here on the same schedule, available around the clock and year round: tree removal, tree trimming, and stump grinding for the leaning oak or the crowded pine you would rather deal with today than during the next warning. When the radar turns red over Cobb, Elite Catastrophe answers, and it answers on the quiet days too.

What we bring to Smyrna

  • 24/7 emergency tree service, when a storm drops a tree on your home

Everyday tree work in Smyrna is available year round, not only after a storm. When severe weather does hit, we are the crew that answers fast and works structures first.
