Storm Damage & Emergency Tree Service in Powder Springs, GA

Powder Springs is growing fast, and much of that growth happened right in among the trees. New neighborhoods here are carved out of wooded ground and set into the heavy red clay that defines this corner of Cobb County, and that combination is what puts the town on watch every spring. Saturated clay loses its grip on a root plate, a straight-line wind leans on an exposed tree, and something that stood straight yesterday is on the house today. Elite Catastrophe is a Rome-based, family-owned company serving Powder Springs, and the storm is what most people are calling about when they first reach us. The trees that cause the most trouble in these newer subdivisions are the leftover pines. They grew up crowded and slender inside a stand, then construction took away the trees around them and left them tall, thin, and exposed, rooted in clay that was graded and packed hard. A wet week plus a hard gust, and those are the pines that snap or uproot onto a roof. When a storm hits Powder Springs, structures come first, always: the tree on the house, the limb over the front door, the trunk blocking the drive, with roof tarping and storm damage cleanup right behind. That is the urgent lane, and it leads for a reason. But the full service runs year round and around the clock: tree removal of what is too far gone, tree trimming to bring broken or overgrown limbs back to safe wood, and stump grinding so the yard is usable again. Storm or clear sky, the crew that answers is the same, and Powder Springs can call it anytime.

What we bring to Powder Springs

  • 24/7 emergency tree service, when a storm drops a tree on your home
  • Storm damage restoration, from the tree to the roof it landed on
  • Emergency roof tarping, to stop the water until repairs are made
  • Tree removal, storm-driven or scheduled, any time of year
  • Tree trimming and pruning, to keep the canopy off your roof
  • Stump grinding and removal, to finish the job clean

Everyday tree work in Powder Springs is available year round, not only after a storm. When severe weather does hit, we are the crew that answers fast and works structures first.
