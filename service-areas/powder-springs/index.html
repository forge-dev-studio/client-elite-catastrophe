Storm Damage & Emergency Tree Service in Powder Springs, GA

Powder Springs is growing fast, and a lot of that growth happened right in among the trees. New neighborhoods here are carved out of wooded ground and set down in the heavy red clay that defines this part of Cobb County, and that combination is what makes the town a storm-damage waiting room every spring. Saturated clay loses its grip on a root plate, a straight-line wind leans on an exposed tree, and something that stood straight yesterday is on the house today. Elite Catastrophe is the crew that drives down from Rome to deal with it. Here is the honest version: we do not run Powder Springs streets day to day. We are a Rome-based, family-owned storm-response company, and we deploy to Powder Springs when severe weather leaves emergency tree work behind. The trees that give us the most trouble in these newer subdivisions are the leftover pines. They grew up crowded and slender inside a stand, then construction took away the trees around them and left them tall, thin, and exposed, rooted in clay that was graded and compacted. A wet week plus a hard gust, and those are the pines that snap or uproot onto a roof. When we get to Powder Springs after a storm, structures come first, always: the tree on the house, the limb over the front door, the trunk blocking the drive. Then we move to removals of what is too far gone, trimming the broken and hanging limbs back to safe wood, and grinding the stumps so the yard is usable again. Severe weather is the reason to call, and Elite Catastrophe takes those calls 24/7.

What we bring to Powder Springs

  • 24/7 emergency tree service, when a storm drops a tree on your home
  • Storm damage restoration, from the tree to the roof it landed on
  • Emergency roof tarping, to stop the water until repairs are made
  • Tree removal, for the hazards a storm leaves standing
