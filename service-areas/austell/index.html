Storm Damage & Emergency Tree Service in Austell, GA

When a storm parks over Austell, the town gets hit two ways at once. Wind and hail work the trees while Sweetwater Creek and the low bottoms take on water, and a tree standing in soft, saturated ground near the creek does not need much of a gust to come over. Austell mixes an older town core with newer growth on the edges, so the hazards run the full range. Elite Catastrophe serves Austell from Rome, a family-owned crew that answers the call and drives to the damage when severe weather turns those trees into emergencies. The flood-prone bottoms are what set Austell apart. Ground that stays wet for days holds trees loosely, and the loblolly pines and creek-side hardwoods rooted in it are prime candidates to uproot when the wind arrives on top of the water. Up in the older neighborhoods, mature oaks over close-set houses fail the way old oaks do, shedding heavy limbs onto rooflines and cars. Whatever brought the tree down, the order of work stays the same when a storm hits Austell: structures first. The trunk through the roof, the limb pinning a vehicle, the tree blocking the only road out of a low-lying street, all of it ahead of anything cosmetic, with roof tarping to hold a damaged roof until repairs can be made. Once the emergency is stable, the everyday work is right there and available around the clock and year round: tree removal, tree trimming back to safe wood, storm damage cleanup, and stump grinding. You do not have to wait for the creek to rise or the sky to open. Elite Catastrophe works Austell on the calm days and the hard ones alike.
