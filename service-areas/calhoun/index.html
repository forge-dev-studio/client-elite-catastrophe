Tree Service in Calhoun, Georgia

Calhoun wears two faces, and your trees depend on which one your house sits in. The Gordon County seat has its established side: in-town streets where water oaks and red oaks have had decades to spread over roofs, sidewalks, and power lines. And it has its I-75 side, where newer neighborhoods keep filling in near the interstate and the trees are either fresh plantings or tall pines left over from whatever grew there before the graders arrived. Both kinds of yard end up needing tree work. They just need it for different reasons. The old-canopy problems in Calhoun are the classic ones. Big hardwoods carrying dead limbs over the driveway. Water oaks that look full and green while the trunk quietly rots from the inside. Trees planted a few feet from the foundation back when they were the size of a broomstick. On the newer side, the usual suspect is the leftover loblolly: tall, thin, shallow-rooted in red clay, and suddenly exposed to wind it never had to stand against before. After a wet week that clay loses its grip, and the spring storms do the rest. Elite Catastrophe is a family-owned tree service based in Rome, and Gordon County sits comfortably inside our regular working range. We bring the same crew and equipment up to Calhoun that we use at home: removals in tight quarters, precision trimming, deadwood cleanup in mature crowns, hazard evaluations for leaners and storm-scarred trunks, and stump grinding that leaves you a lawn instead of a monument. When severe weather crosses Gordon County, our Elite Catastrophe storm crews respond 24/7. For everything short of that, the easiest peace of mind is a calm walk around your Calhoun property with someone who works on trees every single day: which ones are fine, which ones need attention, and which one should come down before it decides on its own.
