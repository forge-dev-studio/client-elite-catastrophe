Storm Damage & Emergency Tree Service in Marietta, GA

In Marietta, the call often comes right after the sky has finished its worst. A summer storm pushes through Cobb County, and one of the big oaks that has shaded a street near the historic square for years finally lets go, landing across a roof or splitting a driveway. That is the moment Elite Catastrophe moves for. We are a family-owned tree and storm-response crew based up in Rome, and we serve Marietta year round, with the storm emergency as the reason most people first call. When a storm hits Marietta, our work is triage before anything else. Structures come first: the tree through the roof, the limb pinning a car in the drive, the trunk blocking the only way in or out. If the roof is opened up, we can tarp it to hold back the next round of weather until storm roof repair can follow. Marietta's older neighborhoods carry some of the densest mature canopy in Cobb. Water oaks and red oaks planted decades ago now stand taller than the houses they shelter, with heavy limbs reaching over rooflines and power lines, and when straight-line winds and hail work them over the failures come fast. None of that is only a storm-day service, though. The everyday tree work is available around the clock and year round: full removals of trees too far gone to save, trimming broken or overgrown limbs back to safe wood, storm damage cleanup, and grinding out the stumps a takedown leaves behind. Whether the radar is red over Cobb County or the sky is clear, Elite Catastrophe answers calls in Marietta and points the trucks toward town.

What we bring to Marietta

  • 24/7 emergency tree service, when a storm drops a tree on your home
  • Storm damage restoration, from the tree to the roof it landed on
  • Emergency roof tarping, to stop the water until repairs are made
  • Tree removal, storm-driven or scheduled, any time of year

  • Stump grinding and removal, to finish the job clean

Everyday tree work in Marietta is available year round, not only after a storm. When severe weather does hit, we are the crew that answers fast and works structures first.
