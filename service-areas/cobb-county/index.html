Storm Response Across Cobb County

Cobb County sits well southeast of Rome, and Elite Catastrophe is not a daily fixture on these streets. What brings us down here is weather. When a spring or summer storm line rolls across metro Atlanta and leaves trees on roofs, across driveways, and down on power lines, our crews load up in Rome and deploy to Cobb to handle the emergency. That is the honest arrangement: a Rome-based, family-owned storm crew that answers the call 24/7 and drives to where the damage is. Cobb carries a heavy tree load, from the mature oaks over older Marietta and Smyrna neighborhoods to the leftover pines in newer south-county subdivisions, so when the wind gets serious there are plenty of targets. We work structures first: the tree through the bedroom, the limb pinning a car, the trunk blocking the only way out. Removals, trimming, and stump grinding are available too, but the reason to keep our number is the storm.
