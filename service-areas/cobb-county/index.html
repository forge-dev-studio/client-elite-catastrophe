Tree Service & Storm Response Across Cobb County

Cobb County sits southeast of Rome, and Elite Catastrophe serves it in full. When a spring or summer storm line rolls across the county and drops trees on roofs, across driveways, and onto power lines, our family-owned crew answers the call and gets on the road to the damage, available around the clock and year round. Storm work is where the urgency lives, so that is what we lead with: structures first, the tree through the roof, the limb pinning a car, the trunk blocking the only way out, plus storm damage cleanup and roof tarping to keep the weather out until repairs can follow. Cobb carries a heavy tree load, from the mature oaks over older Marietta and Smyrna neighborhoods to the leftover pines in newer south-county subdivisions, so the targets are everywhere. The everyday work is here too: tree removal, tree trimming, and stump grinding, whenever Cobb homeowners need it, storm or no storm.
